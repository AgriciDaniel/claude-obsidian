- Triple tax advantage: pre-tax contributions, tax-free growth, tax-free withdrawals for qualified medical expenses
- Employer contributes ~$2,560.25/yr
- Samuel also contributes (annual max for HDHP plan)
- HSA is an investment account — funds invested and compounding

## Other Salesforce Coverage

- **Disability**: Salesforce default plan (short-term and long-term)

## Notes

The HDHP + HSA combination is optimal for someone like Samuel: young, healthy, high income. The HSA functions as a stealth retirement account — triple tax advantage, no "use it or lose it" rule (unlike FSA), and funds accumulate for future medical costs or retirement spending.
