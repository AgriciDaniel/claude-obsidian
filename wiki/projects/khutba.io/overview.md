# khutba.io — Product Overview

## Definition

Live auto-translation + auto-scrolling display system for masjids. Spoken Arabic/English khutba → real-time multilingual subtitles on screens inside the masjid.

Launch: Birmingham, UK. Expand nationally, then globally.

## Core Product

| Layer | What |
|---|---|
| Input | Live mic audio from speaker/pulpit |
| Engine | Speech-to-text + real-time translation |
| Output | Auto-scrolling multilingual text on display screens |
| Languages | Arabic, English, Urdu, Somali, Bengali (expandable) |

## Product Tiers

| Tier | Product | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Core | Live Display Subscription | Paid per masjid, monthly |
| Add-on | Extra Language Pack | Languages beyond base 3 |
| Add-on | Archive Module | Searchable recorded khutba history |
| Funnel | Free First Khutba | Trial converts to paid |

## Strategy

Every masjid decision pushes toward monthly subscription. Free trial converts. Language packs upsell. Archive builds retention.

## Why It Exists

- Non-Arabic speakers (Urdu, Somali, Bengali, English) miss meaning of khutba
- No dedicated affordable product for this market exists
- Masjids rely on volunteers translating manually — unreliable
- High community trust = word-of-mouth growth within Muslim networks
