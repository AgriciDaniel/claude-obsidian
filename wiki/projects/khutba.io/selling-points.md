# khutba.io — Selling Points

## Core Pitch

> "Every Muslim in your masjid understands the khutba — in their language, live, on screen."

## Top 5 Selling Points

### 1. Full congregation inclusion
Urdu, Somali, Bengali, English speakers follow the khutba without a volunteer translator. No extra staff. No delays.

### 2. Zero hardware cost
Works on any existing screen + laptop/tablet. No custom hardware. Plug in, open browser, live.

### 3. Under 10-minute setup
Admin creates account, connects mic, chooses languages. Done before the khutba starts.

### 4. Priced for masjid budgets
Not enterprise SaaS pricing. Community-friendly monthly sub, cancel anytime.

### 5. Archive = institutional memory
Every khutba stored and searchable. New imam, new committee — knowledge stays.

## Objections → Answers

| Objection | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Our imam translates live" | Let him focus on delivery — translation handled |
| "Too expensive" | Less than a box of flyers per month |
| "Tech is unreliable" | Offline fallback mode, local network support |
| "Arabic displays wrong" | RTL built in from day 1 |
| "GDPR / recordings concern" | Opt-in archive, UK data storage |

## Target Customers

- Masjid committees (decision makers)
- Islamic centres with multi-ethnic congregations
- Friday Jumu'ah — largest weekly gathering
- Ramadan tarawih — high attendance, long sessions
- Nikah / Janazah — formal speeches needing translation
