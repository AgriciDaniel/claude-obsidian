## RSU & ESPP Structure

**RSU (Restricted Stock Units)**
- Granted annually via Salesforce Stock Refresh Program
- Standard 4-year vest: 25% at 1 year cliff, then quarterly
- Vest as ordinary income at fair market value on vest date
- Administered through E*TRADE
- 2025 RSU income (W2 Box 14): $49,806

**ESPP (Employee Stock Purchase Plan)**
- Shares purchased at a discount through payroll deduction
- 2025 ESPP gains (W2): $5,079
- Creates cost basis complexity — qualifying vs. disqualifying dispositions
- Reason Samuel uses a CPA (audited once related to this)

## Tax Implications

RSU and ESPP are both included in W2 wages when they vest/are sold. The cost basis tracking for subsequent sales of held shares is complex. AGAPE CPA (Yi Lin) handles this currently. See [[taxes/_index]].

In 2024, Samuel realized $110,112 in capital gains — mostly from liquidating RSU/ESPP shares. Strategy has shifted to selling upon vest/purchase rather than holding.
