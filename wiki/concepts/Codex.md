# Codex

## Tagline

**"Turn complex technical content into high-conversion sales assets"**

## Definition

Technical-content → sales-asset transformer. Takes engineering schematics, instruction manuals, and product specs and outputs how-it-works videos, sales carousels, and competitor battle cards. Helps technical firms and skilled tradesmen sell better.

## How It Works

1. Tradesman or firm uploads technical material (manuals, schematics, spec sheets)
2. Codex extracts mechanism, value prop, differentiators
3. Outputs sales-ready content

## Inputs → Outputs

| Input | Output |
|---|---|
| Instruction manual | How-it-works explainer video |
| Engineering schematic | Sales carousel post |
| Product spec sheet | Competitor battle card |
| Technical whitepaper | Pitch deck slide |

## Why It Matters

Technical tradesmen and firms know their craft but can't sell it. Their manuals look like NASA documents. Customers don't read them. Codex translates technical depth into customer-ready persuasion.

## Funnel Role

Tradesman wins lead via [[Intake Engine]] → uses Codex content to close the sale at higher prices.

## Relationship to Intake

- [[Intake Engine]] = get the lead
- Codex = close the lead with technical authority + clear sales narrative
