# Vicinity

## Tagline

**"Stop letting your best work rot in your camera roll"**

## Definition

Completed-job → marketing-asset converter. Takes photos and details from finished jobs and turns them into WhatsApp-ready and website-ready visual marketing content. Acts like a one-tap marketing agency for tradesmen.

## How It Works

1. Tradesman finishes a job, takes photos
2. Uploads to Vicinity
3. Outputs: before/after carousels, WhatsApp share cards, website portfolio entries, Instagram-ready posts

## Outputs

| Asset | Use |
|---|---|
| Before/after carousel | Instagram, WhatsApp status |
| Job summary card | WhatsApp share to past customers |
| Portfolio entry | Auto-add to tradesman's website |
| Reel-style video | Short social content |

## Why Tradesmen Care

Past work = best marketing they have. But it sits in camera rolls forever. Vicinity automates turning that work into new work.

## Funnel Role

Existing customers see your past jobs → refer you. Cold prospects see the portfolio attached to your [[Intake Engine]] link → convert at higher rate.

## Relationship to Intake

- [[Intake Engine]] link can attach a Vicinity-generated portfolio
- Lead arrives, sees recent work, trusts faster, sends £5k+ enquiry
