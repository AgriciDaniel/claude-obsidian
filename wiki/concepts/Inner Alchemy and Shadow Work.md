# Inner Alchemy and Shadow Work

The practice of turning inward to neutralise the psychological charge created by rising collective shadow, rather than projecting it outward.

> [!key-insight] Core Claim
> The light increases, which forces shadow to surface. Those who cannot do inner work will fragment. Those who can will integrate.

---

## The Mechanism (EYES•IN, Feb 2026)

- "The amount of light anchoring into this realm is forcefully bringing the shadow to the surface. Collectively and individually."
- This increase in light causes inner static charge. If you cannot neutralise it through inner alchemy, it expresses as outer disturbance.
- The shadow's intensity will cause many people to retreat into fragmented identity: survival mode, victim consciousness, projection of anger, villainisation.
- Their demons come out in full force to protect them from seeing the cause of their suffering.

---

## The Warning

> "As the intensity of the shadow comes up really strong, fragmentation of the psyche will cause a form of psychosis to those who are unable to bring coherence to their inner and outer worlds."
> **DO NOT ENGAGE.** Remain connected to God, grounded and discerning while keeping your heart open.

---

## Practice

- Inner alchemy: turn eyes inward, not outward
- Stay grounded and discerning
- Keep the heart open while not engaging with others' projected shadow
- Connection to something larger (God, spirit, meaning) as anchor
