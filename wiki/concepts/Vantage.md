# Vantage

## Tagline

**"Stop losing £1M bids to prettier firms"**

## Definition

Tender → bid deck transformer. Takes tender documents and converts them into high-quality bid decks, 3D renders, and infographics. Lets smaller firms present like tier-1 contractors and win bigger jobs.

## How It Works

1. Tradesman uploads tender documents
2. Vantage extracts spec, requirements, scope
3. Outputs a polished bid pack: deck, 3D visualisation, infographics
4. Tradesman submits — looks like a £10M firm not a 3-man crew

## Inputs → Outputs

| Input | Output |
|---|---|
| Raw tender PDF | Branded bid deck |
| Site spec | 3D render mockups |
| Scope documents | Infographic summaries |

## Why It Matters

Big jobs go to firms with polished decks. Small tradesmen lose bids not because of capability but because of presentation. Vantage closes that visual gap.

## Funnel Role

[[Intake Engine]] gets the tradesman serious leads. Vantage helps them WIN the bigger ones once they have a chance to bid.

## Relationship to Intake

- [[Intake Engine]] = filter junk, get good leads
- Vantage = win the £100k+ leads that come through
