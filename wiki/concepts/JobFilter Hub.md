## What is JobFilter

SaaS tool for UK tradesmen. Filters bad inbound leads, helps win premium jobs, replaces wasted evenings quoting tyre-kickers. Target: sole traders / small teams (1–5 people), UK-based trades, £35k–£80k/yr.

Core monetisation: [[Intake Engine]] subscription. Everything else feeds that.
