# JobFilter Product overview

## Definition

JobFilter is a digital gatekeeper for UK tradesmen. Core product is the [[Intake Engine]] (£49/month). Three advantage layers ([[Vantage]], [[Vicinity]], [[Codex]]) widen the moat. [[Free Tools]] feed the funnel.

## Product Map

| Tier | Product | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Core | [[Intake Engine]] | Filtering link — paid £49/mo |
| Advantage | [[Vantage]] | Lead source intelligence |
| Advantage | [[Vicinity]] | Geographic radius filter |
| Advantage | [[Codex]] | Decision/scoring engine |
| Funnel | [[Free Tools]] | Free utilities driving signups |

## Strategy

Every site decision must push toward [[Intake Engine]] subscription. Free tools, advantages, and content all funnel here. Subscription = single revenue lever.

## Why This Layout

- One paid product = simple pricing, clear value
- Advantages prevent commodity competition (Checkatrade clones)
- Free tools = SEO + acquisition without ad spend
- Tradesman never confused — knows exactly what they're paying for
