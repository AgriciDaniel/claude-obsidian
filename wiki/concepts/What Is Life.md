A collection of one-word answers from history's greatest minds, compiled by [sei].

## Synthesis

- Socrates: life as a series of trials that reveal character
- Gandhi: life as the practice of love, the only force strong enough
- Jobs: life as trusting that dots connect looking backward
- Aristotle: life as the exercise of reason and virtue (eudaimonia)
