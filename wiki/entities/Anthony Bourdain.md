# Anthony Bourdain

Chef, writer, and travel documentarian. Host of *Parts Unknown* and *No Reservations*. Known for his philosophy of authentic travel and presence.

---

## Key Quote (saved Jan 2025)

> "To sit alone or with a few friends, half-drunk under a full moon, you just understand how lucky you are; it's a story you can't tell. It's a story you almost by definition, can't share. I've learned in real time to look at those things and realize: I just had a really good moment."

**Also saved:** Photo of Bourdain with handwritten sign above him: "Be A Traveler Not A Tourist!"

---

## Core Ideas

- Presence over documentation
- The best moments cannot be shared, only experienced
- Travel as genuine encounter, not consumption
