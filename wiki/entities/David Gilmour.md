# David Gilmour

Guitarist and vocalist of Pink Floyd. Known for emotionally expressive guitar work on albums like *Wish You Were Here* and *The Wall*.

---

## Key Quote (saved Mar 2026)

> "It's the emotion in the music that matters, not the technique."

**Context:** Paired with the *Wish You Were Here* album cover (two men shaking hands, one on fire) — a visual of emotional risk in human connection.

---

## Core Ideas

- Emotion as primary in creative work, technique as secondary
- Resonates with [[Boredom Is the New IQ]]: depth over performance
