# Acura Integra Type S

## Decision

**Buying it.** Decision made after extensive research and deliberation with Claude. Purchase in progress as of mid-2026.

## Why the ITS Won

Criteria: manual transmission, sports/performance, 4 doors (for future family), sharp exterior without boy-racer look, safety features, under ~$60k.

**ITS was the only car that checked all boxes.** Direct quote from research: *"If your criteria are similar to mine — Manual, sports/performance car, 4 doors to easily drive kids around, sharp sporty exterior, extensive safety features, and under about $60k — I'm not sure there's a better option out there."*

### vs. Lexus IS 350 F Sport

| | ITS | IS 350 F Sport |
|--|-----|----------------|
| Transmission | Manual (6-speed) | Auto only |
| Drive | FWD | RWD |
| Interior | Modern | Outdated camera/interior |
| Shifter feel | Excellent, rev-match | "Slow as molasses" |
| Daily comfort | Comfort mode smooth | "Drives like a refrigerator" |
| Reliability | Honda quality | Bulletproof |
| Exhaust | Nice stock exhaust | Borla aftermarket option |

Verdict: IS 350 is more reliable but soul-sapping. ITS wins on engagement. Samuel's decision: manual driving experience > RWD + reliability crutch.

### Compared to Previous Car (BRZ / GR86)

Much bigger, more interior room, stronger chassis, far safer. Better shifter feel and clutch. More comfortable daily. Quicker. Family-capable. The BRZ was the right learning car; the ITS is the right life car.

## Security Plan

Context: Samuel previously had two cars stolen (Infiniti G35 S and a '21 Honda Type R). Security is a high priority on this purchase.

Recommended stack for ITS in the Bay Area / Peninsula:

| Layer | Component | Cost | Why |
|-------|-----------|------|-----|
| Primary | IGLA 231 immobilizer | $1,200–$1,400 | CAN-bus engine block; stops drive-away theft |
| Port | BEM Auto "Tune Saver" OBD lock (metal) | $80–$100 | Prevents key cloning via OBD port |
| Wheels | Advanced wheel locks M14x1.5, 60° conical seat | $150 | Defeats standard extractor sockets |
| Tracking | Pebblebee Clip (Android, no monthly fee) | $30 | Silent tracker; clip the speaker wire and hide in trunk/carpet |
| Visual | The Club (yellow) | $40 | Visual deterrent |

- IGLA installer: Sound Innovations (Union City) or West Coast Auto Security (mobile)
- Use Pebblebee not AirTag (Android ecosystem, no monthly fee, Google Find My Device)
